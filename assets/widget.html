Disappearing ice

Danish researchers from the University of Copenhagen and Aarhus University photographed glaciers in east Greenland in 2010 from the same vantage point used by scientists in 1933. Move the sliders below each image to the right to see how much two glaciers have retreated because of the warming climate.
